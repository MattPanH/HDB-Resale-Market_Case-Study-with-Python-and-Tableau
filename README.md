<h2> Introduction </h2>
I am an analyst at HDB and has just received a series of questions from senior management. I am tasked with the development of interactive dashboards, predictive models, and policy analysis using HDB resale market data from data.gov.sg. or any other reliable sources. Upon completion, I will present a summary of my findings and insights to HDB senior management.
<br>
<br>
<h2> Problem Statement </h2>
To visualise HDB resale market data via interactive Tableau dashboards, predict HDB resale flat characteristics via machine learning models, and analyse the HDB resale market using statistical techniques and models. <br>
<br>
<h2> Objectives </h2>
1) To equip potential buyers of HDB resale flats with the necessary information to make informed decisions via interactive dashboards.<br> 
2) To inform HDB if a resale flat transaction fits market expectations through machine learning models.<br> 
3) To evaluate HDB housing policies through a data-driven and evidence-based approach.<br> 
<br>
<h2> Target Audience </h2>
1)	A deputy director from HDB senior management, thinking of revising their HDB housing policies. <br>
2)	A newly married couple, trying to buy a HDB resale flat at an affordable price at their preferred location. <br>
3)	A homeowner looking to sell their HDB flat at a competitive price to interested parties. <br>
<br>
<h2> Data Sources </h2>
1) HDB Resale Flat Data from data.gov.sg <br>
2) Singapore School Data from data.gov.sg <br>
3) Geospatial Data from Onemap’s Geocoding APIs <br>
3) COE Price Data from data.gov.sg <br>
All datasets have been uploaded on the repository for easy reference.
